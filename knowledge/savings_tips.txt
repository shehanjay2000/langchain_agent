Saving Money Tips

Build an emergency fund equal to 3 to 6 months of living expenses.

Pay yourself first by saving at least 20% of your income every month.

Avoid high-interest debt such as credit card balances.

Cook at home more often instead of eating out.

Cancel subscriptions you rarely use.

Buy items in bulk when they are discounted.

Use public transport or carpool to reduce fuel costs.

Set clear savings goals such as travel, education, or a house deposit.

Track daily small expenses because small costs add up over time.

Use automatic bank transfers to move money into savings accounts regularly.

Saving consistently is more important than saving large amounts occasionally.
